Digital FlipBook Creation

Innovatively digitizing presentations, reports and documentations.

Our FlipBook Concept is a highly innovative medium of digitizing and animating reports or documentations of any type or format. It involves  transforming presentations from the traditional printed material documents to a highly digitized and animated format. The Flipbook increases engagement and interactivity, particularly encountering special features in its creation. 

SoftFlip Flipbook enables the creative application of animations, audios, videos, links, youtube, flash and image  albums in every creation if required for both online and offline publications

This digitized media which is perfectly responsive to different screen sizes and can be viewed from any device platform can be deployed in a variety of ways some of which include:

  1. Physical Output Media - eg Flash or Pen Drives, Cds etc.
  2. Live Website - It can be deployed on a live website and accessed by different devices from the web address.
  3. Mobile - The digitized flipbook can also built and deployed specifically for mobile devices.
